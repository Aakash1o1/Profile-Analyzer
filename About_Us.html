What We Do

We specialize in providing detailed analysis of your coding journey, starting with LeetCode. By linking your LeetCode account to our platform, we display your performance in a visually accessible calendar format, enabling you to:

  • Track your problem-solving streaks.
  • Visualize your progress over time.
  • Spot patterns in your learning habits.
